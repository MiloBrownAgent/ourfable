## Overview

Personalized children's books have strong seasonal demand tied to gifting occasions. Peak buying periods: Christmas/Hanukkah (40% of annual revenue for competitors), birthdays (year-round, ~30%), and other holidays (~30%). As a digital product with instant delivery, OurFable has a unique advantage: last-minute gift buyers can still purchase on the day of the occasion.
