# Our Fable Landing Pages Redesign Plan

Audience
- /reserve: parents considering starting this for their child
- /gift: gifters buying a meaningful family gift
- /for-your-family: warm traffic who already understand the product from another family

Job
- /reserve: make reservation feel emotionally important, concrete, safe, and obvious
- /gift: make gifting feel obvious, emotionally strong, and socially graceful
- /for-your-family: convert "I've seen this from the inside" into "I want this for my own family"

Message hierarchy
- shared system first: premium, calm, intimate, private-by-design, family-first, enduring
- /reserve: child-first emotional promise -> why now -> what you're reserving -> what happens next -> trust -> proof slot -> close CTA
- /gift: meaningful gift promise -> why it matters -> what the family receives -> why better than normal gift -> objection handling -> trust -> close CTA
- /for-your-family: insider recognition -> what they already know from the inside -> why it matters for their own child -> how simple it is -> trust -> warm proof -> close CTA

Section structure
- /reserve
  1. editorial hero with left promise / right reserve card
  2. why reserve now
  3. what you're reserving visual blocks
  4. 3-step what happens next
  5. trust / founder / privacy section
  6. proof placeholder section
  7. bottom CTA close
- /gift
  1. gift-specific hero
  2. why this is a meaningful gift
  3. what the family receives
  4. why this is better than a normal baby gift
  5. objection / Q&A section
  6. founder / trust / privacy section
  7. bottom gift CTA close
- /for-your-family
  1. warm-context hero
  2. what you already know from the inside
  3. why it matters more for your own child
  4. how simple it is to begin
  5. trust / founder / privacy section
  6. warm proof placeholder section
  7. bottom CTA close

CTA strategy
- one primary CTA per page
- one supporting action max
- logo always returns to homepage
- /reserve gets gift escape hatch
- /gift gets parent-start escape hatch
- /for-your-family gets homepage return and reserve action

Shared system rules
- same typography, color, spacing, button style, trust tone, and pacing across all 3 pages
- no generic form-card-only compositions
- no layout clones with copy swaps
- mobile and desktop both intentionally composed
- proof zones must exist without breaking layout when empty
